How It Works

Three Steps. Every Answer.

From raw parcel data to verified zoning classification in seconds.

1

Ingest Parcels

BCPAO centroids for all 252K parcels in Brevard County feed into the spatial index. SR2881 coordinate system for sub-meter accuracy.

2

AI Classifies

Each parcel centroid queries the municipality's GIS layer. The AI waterfall (Firecrawl to Gemini to Claude) resolves zoning codes into human-readable categories.

3

Instant Answers

Search any address. Get zoning category, permitted uses, density limits, and overlay districts in under 100ms. Color-coded map included.
